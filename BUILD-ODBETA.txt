To build for od-beta change scripts 'configure-odbeta.sh' and 'makefile-odbeta.sh" to put your toolchain path then execute:

./configure-odbeta.sh
./make-odbeta.sh

In my current toolchain these are the enabled decoders list:
Opus enabled
mpg123 enabled
Vorbis enabled
FLAC enabled
Speex enabled
ModPlug enabled
